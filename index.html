our blogs

«ProModelAgency»

The work of the division is aimed at selecting models for various events. The model is the embodiment of beauty and elegance, so this profession expands its boundaries and is not limited only to the demonstration of clothes or photo shoots.

«ProEventAgency»

The unit's task is completely focused on the organization of the celebration, from the first chapters of the script, right up to the final chords. The center's specialists provide a full package of services for the creation of any celebrations - weddings, anniversaries, presentations, as well as corporate events.

«ProMusic Audio Production»

The activity is dedicated to professional recordings of songs and instrumental compositions. For young and creative singers and musicians, this is a unique opportunity to make their debut in the world of music. Also, one way to do this is to create a dance group.
